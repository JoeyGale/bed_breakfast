At Simpson Bed and Breakfast, we welcome everyone to visit and enjoy our fresh homemade food.

We have fabulous options for housing and breakfast that will make you want to come back for more.

It was founded by Christopher Shima and Alyssa Pingel in the year of 2018.

Our staff will make your stay feel like home.



Directions to our Bed and Breakfast

From Northwest Iowa

Take highway 20 to I-35. Then exit on to highway 69 which will bring you into Indianola.

Finally take the first left and you will arrive at Simpson Bed and Breakfast.


From the West Des Moines area.

Take I-235 south and continue on it until reaching exit 68. Take that exit onto highway 5 and then continue until reaching exit 70A for Indianola.

After taking that exit, continue on that road and take the first right and you will arrive.

When you arrive at campus, there are maps around the outside to help you navigate through campus and figure out which buildings are located where. Check-in is located at the Kent Campus center right outside of Kresge housing. After checking in, you will receive your room pass to be able to access the housing you will be staying in.
