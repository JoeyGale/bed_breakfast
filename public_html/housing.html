On-Campus Bed and Breakfast

Housing


Room in Kresge

Double room with Two Full Beds

This room is equipped with two full beds that can rearranged either as bunk beds or separated.

It also has fully functioning air conditioning that you can change to fit your comfort.

Laundry is provided on every level of Kresge free of charge.




Room in Barker

Double room with Two Full Beds

This room is equipped with two full beds that can rearranged either as bunk beds, lofts, or separated. All at your convenience.

It also has fully functioning air conditioning that you can change to fit your comfort. Also classified as geothermal heating and cooling.

Barker rooms have been recently renovated and have larger closets if you plan to stay longer.

Laundry is provided in the basement of Barker free of charge.




Room in Station Square

Double Room with Two Full Beds

Station Square rooms contain two full beds that can be separated, lofted, or bunked as well as the others listed.

Although, A room in Station Square is equipped with it's own bathroom that you share with one roommate and has a personal breakfast buffet you can attend at will.

New carpets have been added to certain rooms. You will be provided clean towels and sheets for your stay at Station.
