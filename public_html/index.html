Welcome to our bed and breakfast!


We have two available housing units that are located in Indianola, IA.
Kresge Hall is the oldest and largest housing unit, was build in 1949, an addition in 1961, and was newly renovated in 2001.
Barker Hall was built in 1954, renovated in 2002, and had an addition in Fall 2005.
Both of these buildings are smoke free.
