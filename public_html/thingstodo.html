Things To Do

Everyone staying at Simpson Bed & Breakfast is more than welcome to partake in any of the activities Simpson College has to offer.
Simpson College constantly has events from workouts to paintings, to check what events are planned coming up, please visit the website at
Simpson College Events

Football Games


Simpson College has varsity football games throughout the fall on Saturdays and JV games on the week days. The following link will take you to their schedule.

Simpson College Football Schedule

Bingo


Simpson College has bingo nights open to the public where you can win anything from a jar of pickles to a TV and soundbar. The next one will be September 14th at 9pm.
For more information contact Simpson College and they will direct you to the event director.
