Football Games

Simpson College has varsity football games throughout the fall on Saturdays and JV games on the week days. The following link will take you to their schedule.

Simpson College Football Schedule Simpson also has homecoming events in October during homecoming week. There are events like "Yell like hell" and the homecoming dance. These are only a couple of examples during homecoming. Make sure you come to the football game and support the Simpson Storm!

Bingo

Simpson College has bingo nights open to the public where you can win anything from a jar of pickles to a TV and soundbar. The next one will be September 14th at 9pm.

Mentalist

The Mentalist is a guy that can perform what seems to be magic tricks. He reads your mind and amazes the crowd with his clever and unbelievable tricks. Sean will be in Hubbel on October 4th at 8pm.

For more information contact Simpson College and they will direct you to the event director.
